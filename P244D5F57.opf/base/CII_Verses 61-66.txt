pūrvānubhūtanaṣṭebhyaḥ kiṃ me sāram avasthitam |yeṣu me ’bhiniviṣṭena gurūṇāṃ laṅghitaṃ vacaḥ
||61||

jīvalokam imaṃ tyaktvā bandhūn paricitāṃs tathā |ekākī kvāpi yāsyāmi kiṃ me sarvaiḥ priyāpriyaiḥ
||62||

iyam eva tu me cintā yuktā rātrin divaṃ sadā |aśubhān niyataṃ duḥkhaṃ niḥsareyaṃ tataḥ katham
||63||

mayā bālena mūḍhena yat kiṃcit pāpam ācitam |prakṛtyā yac ca sāvadyaṃ prajñaptyāvadyam eva ca
||64||

tatsarvaṃ deśayāmy eṣa nāthānām agrataḥ sthitaḥ |kṛtāñjalir duḥkhabhītaḥ praṇipatya punaḥ punaḥ
||65||

atyayam atyayatvena pratigṛhṇantu nāyakāḥ |na bhadrakam idaṃ nāthā na karttavyaṃ punar mayā
||66||

bodhicaryāvatāre pāpadeśanā dvitīyaḥ paricchedaḥ ||
